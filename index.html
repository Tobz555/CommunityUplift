Welcome to Uplift Community Church

Founded in 2024, Uplift Community Church is a vibrant new faith community in Nairobi. We are a diverse group united by our love for Christ and commitment to serving others.

Our mission is to uplift individuals and communities through the transformative power of the Gospel, fostering spiritual growth, and making a positive impact in our society.

2024

Uplift Community Church officially launched in Nairobi with our first service.
